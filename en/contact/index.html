editX.vn is available via email and messaging 24/7, always ready to support authors at any time. We respond as quickly as possible because we understand the pressures involved in the research and publication process.

📌 We are committed to adhering to strict principles to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and research ethics throughout the scientific manuscript editing process.

Absolute Confidentiality

  • All manuscripts and authors’ personal information are strictly protected.
  • All editors sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) to ensure unpublished research is not leaked or misused.

Respect for Research Ethics

  • We only improve language and structure without interfering with data or research results.
  • We ensure scientific integrity, do not distort content, and do not support plagiarism or academic misconduct, strictly following the guidelines of the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE).
  • The author’s final decision is respected at every stage of manuscript development.

Transparency and Responsibility

  • Always provide clear and transparent feedback on all edits.
  • All software and illustrations used are from licensed or open-source sources.
  • Ensure a professional workflow, meet deadlines, and provide full support to authors during manuscript finalization.

📌 These principles allow us to build a professional and trustworthy service that supports authors on their publication journey.
